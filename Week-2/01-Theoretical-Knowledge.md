## 1. Vulnerability Scanning Techniques

### Core Concepts
- **Network Scanning**: Identifies open ports and services (e.g., Nmap)
- **Application Scanning**: Detects web vulnerabilities (e.g., Nikto)
- **Authenticated vs Unauthenticated Scans**
- **False Positives**: Findings validated manually
